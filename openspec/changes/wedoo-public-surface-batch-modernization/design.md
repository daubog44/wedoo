## Design Strategy

Use Figma where a coherent frame or export exists. Where Figma is stale, infer from:

1. adjacent Wedoo public routes already modernized
2. `DESIGN.md`
3. real captures showing current weaknesses

Keep the result editorial and spacious rather than novelty-first.

## Validation

- real desktop capture
